History

  Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate adventurers and seasoned river guides, Turbo Rafting Extraordinaires was born out of a shared love for white water rafting and the thrill of conquering some of the most challenging rapids in the world. What started as a small, local operation quickly gained a reputation for offering high-energy, exhilarating rafting experiences that catered to thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike.

  With a focus on safety, fun, and exceptional service, Turbo Rafting Extraordinaires grew rapidly, attracting adventurers from all over. The company's unique approach combined top-tier equipment, expert guides, and a relentless commitment to making each trip a memorable adventure. As their reputation for excellence spread, so did their reach, with the company expanding to offer guided trips on some of the most iconic and adrenaline-pumping rivers.

  Today, Turbo Rafting Extraordinaires is known not only for its thrilling white water experiences but also for its dedication to environmental stewardship, providing educational opportunities on the importance of protecting our natural waterways. With a decade of experience, the company remains a leader in the industry, continuing to offer extraordinary rafting experiences that keep customers coming back for more.
